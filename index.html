About Me

I've always been drawn to how systems work beneath the surface — the data they generate, the signals they hide, and how those signals can guide real decisions. That curiosity led me from Electrical & Electronics Engineering into the world of AI and applied data systems.

Over time, I've specialized in building end-to-end data-backed solutions that don't just exist in a notebook — they run in production and support real operations. What drives me is simple: turn messy, human-generated data into intelligent systems that help people work smarter. I enjoy the full lifecycle, from understanding the problem and exploring the data to designing pipelines, validating models, and shipping something people can rely on.
